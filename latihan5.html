About Ubisoft

Ubisoft Entertainment SA (Ubisoft), is involved in the production, publishing, distribution, and operation of video games for various platforms including consoles, PC, and mobile devices. The company's product portfolio includes a wide range of video games such as Assassin’s Creed, The Crew, Far Cry, For Honor, and Just Dance, among others. The company's products cater to a diverse customer base of gamers across different age groups and interests, providing entertainment and interactive experiences.
